INTRODUCTION

Article about David Kovacs and his music

I'm David Kovacs, a Budapest (HU) based DJ/Producer, active for more than 12 years now. -(formerly known as Bonvibe)-
'David Kovacs' being my actual name as a person, my stage name as a DJ & also one of the most common names in my home country (separately & combined as well) - I'm regarding it more like a version of 'John Doe'... an anonymous... a face in the crowd.
Many others in the history of electronic music wore flashy helmets and masks to hide the individual self, thus the focus can be on their music, their craft.
For me, my name is my helmet.
It's like a secret code to earn entrance into a club known only by a few - YOU are part of the few!
I'm not USING music to speak in my behalf.
I LET the music speak for itself.
It's unstoppable... aiming to conquer your hearts one by one.
It takes time but music is timeless.
It's here to stay.
I'm here to stay.
